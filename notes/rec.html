Recommendations

Since I've read some 1500 odd books over the years, I occassionally get asked which were good. I'm not picky, I did read all 6 volumes of "Fat Vampire."

So I'm not sure if the below are actually "good" or "great" but I do count myself lucky that I found them when I did, they've influenced my thought or my life in one way or another.

Things I've Read More Than Once, On Purpose

  • Shadowplay, Nigel Findley
  • My Side of the Mountain, Jean George
  • Dune, Frank Herbert
  • Kings of Infinite Space, James Hynes
  • How the Dead Dream, Lydia Millet
  • After Dark, Haruki Murakami
  • Bleeding Edge, Thomas Pynchon
  • Aftermath, Brian Shawver
