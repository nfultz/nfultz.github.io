Imagine you have three columns, monthly statement date, cash deposited, final balance.

To calc Internal rate of return, do

=XIRR({$E$2:E142;-F142}, {$B$2:B142;B142})

the curly braces will construct arrays. Note the negative sign on F142 - this is as if you did a full withdrawal. the $ anchors are such that this can be filled down over the whole set of data.
